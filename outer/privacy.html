Reddix Privacy Policy

Your data, your control. Effective September 2025.

At Reddix, we believe that privacy is a right. We want to empower you to be the master of your identity. This policy explains what little information we collect and how we use it to improve our services for everyone.

We Collect Minimal Information

We only collect what is necessary to operate Reddix. You don't need to use your real name, and we don't track your precise location. Our goal is a great service, not tracking your every move.

You Are in Control

You decide what to share. Reddix is a public platform, and your posts and profile are visible. You can browse anonymously without an account. You have full control over your presence.

How We Use Data

Any data we collect is used primarily to provide and improve our Services. We focus on allowing people to form communities. We do not sell your personal data to third-party data brokers.

Data Security is a Priority

We use modern encryption and secure protocols to protect your information from unauthorized access. Protecting your data is fundamental to earning and keeping your trust.
