Reddix Rules

Our guiding principles for a healthy community.

Reddix is a vast network of communities created and run by users like you. Through these communities, you can connect with people who share your interests. We encourage you to find—or even create—your home on Reddix.

Rule 1: Remember the Human

Reddix is a place for creating community and belonging, not for attacking marginalized or vulnerable groups. Everyone has a right to use Reddix free of harassment, bullying, and threats of violence.

Rule 2: Abide by Community Rules

Post authentic content into communities where you have a personal interest, and do not cheat or engage in content manipulation like spamming, vote manipulation, or ban evasion.

Rule 3: Respect the Privacy of Others

Instigating harassment, for example by revealing someone's personal or confidential information, is not allowed. Never post or threaten to post intimate or sexually-explicit media of someone without their consent.

Rule 4: Do Not Post or Encourage Illegal Content

Do not post or encourage the posting of content that is illegal or prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, content that facilitates transactions for prohibited goods or services.

Rule 5: Keep it Legal and Avoid Impersonation

Do not post content that is illegal, and don't impersonate an individual or an entity in a misleading or deceptive manner. This helps maintain trust and authenticity on the platform.
